# Auto-Assigner for ServiceNow

**Auto-Assigner** is a scoped application for the **ServiceNow** platform. It
automatically distributes unassigned ServiceNow tickets — incidents, requests,
tasks, and any other task-based record — across the available members of an
assignment group, fairly and on a schedule, with shifts, breaks, working hours,
and round-robin balancing.

It installs into your ServiceNow instance and runs from a ServiceNow Service
Portal page; no external services are involved.
